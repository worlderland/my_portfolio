About

Hi! I'm Alessandro

I am a professional with over 10 years’ experience in computing/IT helpdesk, programming, and electrical engineering. I always aim to achieve objectives to the best of my capabilities and perform assigned tasks according to my leaders’ expectations.

At Purple Circle Financial Services, as part of a small team of 2 developers, my role consists of developing and implementing new features and enhancing existing ones on the portal used by both our admin department and our clients (brokers); the system structure is based on the Laravel PHP framework and Bootstrap for the front-end UI. I am also dedicated to IT helpdesk on a daily basis, assisting other staff members with their Windows/system issues, as well as develop Wordpress based websites for our clients.

In my previous position, as a Supervisor and Customer Service manager at ASA Flexible Doors I led the company’s rapid door department, looking after a team of three technicians, managing supplies, production, service and maintenance, while taking care of over a hundred customers WA wide.

Before joining ASA Flexible Doors in 2014, I moved from Italy to Australia, having an amazing year working and traveling on my first Working Holiday Visa. Travelling is one of my biggest passions along with sports and gaming. I started to feel the “travel bug” in my late teens and it basically became my job in the company I worked for, Dromont Comec Group, where I was a FIFO PLC & SCADA software programmer. Thanks to them I have been almost all over the World, including Australia, which I have later decided to call home.

I am truly passionate about my work and always eager to learn new things. This is the reason why I have invested and dedicated two years to attend a Web Development course at North Metropolitan TAFE in Perth while working part-time at ASA Flexible Doors, to drive my career towards what I love doing.
