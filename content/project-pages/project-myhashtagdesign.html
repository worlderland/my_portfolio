My Hashtag Design

Visit the website

This project is a favour I made for a very close friend of mine; she has a great passion about interior design as well as writing. When she wanted to join them 2, My Hashtag Design was born.

My role in this project had been to go through all the steps someone new to the web world needed to know; therefore, as a consultant, I started from the beginning setting her up on my server to make her familiar with Wordpress (probably the best CMS for Bloggers on today's market), explaining her what was needed. I took her through the millions of themes, giving her few guidelines about how to pick one (and that means not only how pretty it looks...).

She is now devoted to her blog and every time she has some spare time, she sits down, writes and updates it.
